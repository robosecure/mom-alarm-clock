How It Works

Two phones, one morning routine, zero arguments.

📱

Cross-Device Control

Guardian sets alarms. They fire on the child’s phone. Real-time sync via Firebase.

🧮

Must-Verify Alarms

Math quizzes, step counting, QR scan. Can’t just tap dismiss.

✅

Guardian Approval

Review proof. Approve from your lock screen. Or send back to re-verify.

🎤

Voice Alarm

Record a personal “good morning” that plays when the alarm fires.

🔥

Streaks & Rewards

Points for on-time wake-ups. Milestones. Redeemable rewards.

📶

Works Offline

Alarms fire without internet. Actions queue and sync on reconnect.

👪

Up to 4 Children

Per-child alarms, voice clips, rewards, and settings. One guardian app.

⏩

Skip Tomorrow

Swipe left on any alarm. One gesture for sick days and holidays.

🔔

Lock Screen Actions

Approve or deny from push notification. No need to open the app.

🌿

Calm Mode

Brief breathing moment before verification. Gentler mornings.

⚙️

Tomorrow Overrides

One-tap adjust difficulty, method, timer. Auto-clears after one session.

♿

Accessible

VoiceOver labels, 48pt+ touch targets. Designed for bleary-eyed kids.
