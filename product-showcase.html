How It Works

Three steps. Under two minutes. No ongoing effort required on good mornings.

1

Guardian Sets It Up

Create an account, add your child (name + age), set a wake-up time. Quiz verification and auto-complete are the defaults. Share a join code to pair.

2

Child Proves They're Up

The alarm fires on the child's device. They complete a short challenge — age-appropriate math quiz, step counting, or photo — to dismiss it.

3

Good Mornings Run Themselves

By default, the alarm clears once verified. No guardian action needed. You're only notified when something needs attention — failed verification, tamper events, or manual review mode.

Features

Everything that ships in v1.0.

🔒

Two-Device Control

Guardian sets alarms from their phone. Child receives them on their device. Real-time sync via Firebase Firestore.

🧠

Age-Aware Quiz

Math questions adapt to the child's age group (5-7, 8-10, 11-13, 14+). Younger kids get simpler problems with more time. Older kids get multiplication.

🚶

Step Verification

Uses the device pedometer to count steps. The child must physically get out of bed and walk to dismiss the alarm.

📷

Photo Verification

Child submits a photo for the guardian to review. Always requires guardian approval — never auto-acknowledged.

📍

Geofence Verification

Child must reach a designated location (kitchen, bathroom) to dismiss. Uses coarse location, not GPS tracking.

🎤

Voice Alarm

Guardian records a personal wake-up message. Cached on the child's device for offline playback. Falls back to standard sound if removed.

✅

Trust Mode (Default)

Alarm auto-completes when the child verifies. Guardian is only notified when something needs attention. Exception-based parenting.

👀

Strict Mode

Child waits for guardian approval every morning. Guardian reviews verification proof and approves, denies, or escalates.

🔥

Streaks & Rewards

+15 pts for on-time first try, +5 no-snooze bonus, streak milestones at 3/7/14 days. Server-authoritative — no tampering.

📡

Push Notifications

Guardian gets pushed for pending reviews and tamper events. Approve/deny directly from the notification with action buttons.

✈

Works Offline

Alarms fire from local notifications. Verification completes locally. Offline actions queue and sync when connectivity returns.

👫

Up to 4 Children

One guardian account manages up to 4 children. Each child has their own alarms, verification settings, and streaks.

📈

Escalation System

If the child ignores the alarm: gentle reminder → guardian notified → entertainment apps blocked → full app lock. Configurable per alarm.

📅

Tomorrow Overrides

Guardian can adjust difficulty, method, or timer for just the next morning. Auto-clears after one completed session.

🚫

Skip Tomorrow

Swipe an alarm to skip it for one day (sick days, holidays). Auto-resets the following day.

🔓

Account Deletion

In-app deletion cascades the entire family: profiles, sessions, voice clips, join codes. GDPR/CCPA compliant.

Privacy & Safety

What we collect, why, and how we protect it.

🔒 No Tracking

NSPrivacyTracking is false. No advertising identifiers. No ATT framework. No data sold or shared with third parties.

🚫 No Ads

Zero advertising in the app. No ad SDKs. Children never see promotional content.

👶 COPPA Compliant

Children cannot create accounts. Guardian must add each child with explicit consent. Age stored as number, not DOB.

🗑 Full Deletion

Account deletion cascades: auth, user doc, family, children, sessions, tamper events, voice clips, join codes. Nothing retained.

🔐 Encrypted at Rest

Firestore encrypts server data. Local files use iOS File Protection. Parent PIN in Keychain with salted SHA-256.

🛡 App Check

Firebase App Check with App Attest verifies requests come from legitimate app installations. Staged enforcement.
