How It Works

Two phones, one morning routine, zero arguments.

📱

Cross-Device Control

Guardian sets alarms from their phone. Alarms fire on the child's phone. Both stay synced in real-time.

🧮

Must-Verify Alarms

Child can't just tap dismiss. They solve math problems, take steps, or complete a quiz to prove they're awake.

✅

Guardian Approval

Review the proof, approve with a note, or send them back to try again. You stay in the loop.

🎤

Voice Alarm

Record a personal "good morning" message that plays when the alarm fires. Better than any beep.

🔥

Streaks & Rewards

Kids earn points for on-time wake-ups. Build streaks, hit milestones, redeem in the reward store.

📶

Works Offline

Alarms fire even without internet. Actions queue and sync when back online. Never miss a wake-up.
